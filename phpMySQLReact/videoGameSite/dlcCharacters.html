6 Rumored DLC Characters In SFV Post-Launch Posted on Oct. 27

Data Reveals Familiar Faces

From the result of some hackers cracking through files from the SFV Beta, discovery of DLC characters has been made. Here are some of videos revealing the information:

6 Characters Revealed To Be In SFV

Guile

Guile is from the Street Fighter II roster and is a great addtion to the roster. From the perspective of the story, adding him would complete his search for Charlie Nash (also in the game), who has been missing since Street Fighter Alpha 3.

Ibuki

Ibuki once again has made it to another main series sequel of Street Fighter. Quite popular in her Street Fighter III days, fans will rejoice.

Juri

Juri is a fan favorite from Street Fighter IV, although she technically didn't make her debut until Super Street Fighter IV. Surely her popularity has been noted by Capcom and thus has led her to making an appearance in SFV.

Balrog

Balrog is one of the original 4 bosses from Street Fighter II. There's a severe lack of boxing characters in SFV, so having him would be a great add to the roster.

Alex

The titular character from SFIII, Alex has been demanded for quite some time. Although it's kind of strange he didn't make the main initial roster, I'm sure fans will be ecstatic about this one.

Urien

Yet another fan favorite Street Fighter III character long over due for an appearance. Urien is the brother of SF III boss, Gill. Having him in SFV would bridge the gap between SFII and SFIII with the ascenscion of the Illuminati, and possibly explain Charlie Nash's gem on his forehead. Here's hoping he retains his Supert Art, Aegis Reflector.
