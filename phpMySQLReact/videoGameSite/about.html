About Us

GameScoop was the idea of Adam Salcido, Shana Olton, Elham Elzaki to bring video game enthusiasts the latest news and greatest videos from the video game community. By doing this, they hoped to expand people's interest in video games and to grow the community to new heights. While the site is fairly new, the journey has only begun for ourselves and our users. GameScoop is here to bring the best of the video game community!
